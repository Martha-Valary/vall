The Akan Name Finder

This is a website that helps to determine your name based on the day of the week one was born. Akan Naming is a Ghanian system,it generaes both male and female names.
If you are a male,you will be named as follows;

Sunday-Kwasi, Monday-Kwadwo, Tuesday-Kwabena, Wednesday-Kwaku, Thursday-Yaw, Friday-Kofi, Saturday-Kwame

Else if you are female;

Sunday-Akosua, Monday-Adwoa, Tuesday-Abenaa, Wednesday-Akua, Thursday-Yaa, Friday-Afua, Saturday-Ama
